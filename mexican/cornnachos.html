Loaded Black Beans and Corn Nachos

This video shows you how to make loaded nachos

Refried Black Beans

Ingredients

2 tablespoons Loaded Nachosded
½ onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic for Refried Black Beans, chopped
½ chipotle in adobo, chopped
½ teaspoon adobo sauce
1 15-oz. can black beans
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
Kosher salt
Freshly ground pepper

Remaining Ingredients:
1 bag Tortilla chips
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 jalapeño, thinly sliced
Thinly sliced radishes (for serving)
2 cups pico de gallo
½ cup charred corn
Scallions, thinly sliced
Cilantro
Instructions for the black beans:
1: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic, and chipotle in adobo, and cook, stirring often, until softened, about 3 minutes. Transfer onion mixture to a food processor and add black beans, lime juice, adobo sauce, season with salt and pepper. Process until smooth.
2: Heat up another tablespoon of oil in the same pan. Add back in the pureed black beans and fry for 1-2 minutes until slightly thickened. Remove from the pan and set aside in a covered bowl until ready to serve.

To Assemble:
1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
2: Arrange half of chips on a baking sheet. Top with half of beans, then half of each cheese.
3: Repeat layering with remaining chips, beans, and cheese and corn.
4: Bake until cheese is melted and gooey, about 5 minutes.
5: Serve nachos topped with pico de gallo, sliced jalapeño, scallions, cilantro and radishes.
