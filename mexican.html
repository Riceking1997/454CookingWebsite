Recipes

Mexican cuisine varies based on the region due to climate and ethnic differences. We have Northern Mexico which is known its meat dishes and flour tortillas. There's the Pacific Coast & Baja California Penisula where seafood primarily dominates the dishes. Then we have the Oaxaca region known for dem tasty tamales. The three main staples in all Mexican dishes really involve corn, beans and hot peppers. Avocados, tomatoes, and rice are also used heavily in Mexican cuisine.
